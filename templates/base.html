FY1 Rota -> Google Calendar Converter

If you are an FY1 doctor in the UK, use this site to convert your rota into a csv file which can be imported into Google Calendar. Simply:

  1. Select your hospital and specialty/department on the home page
  2. Upload your rota as an Excel file
  3. Specify your row/column and any other specifics
  4. Click 'convert!' and your converted rota will download as a converted csv file
  5. Congrats, you can now import the converted rota into Google calendar
