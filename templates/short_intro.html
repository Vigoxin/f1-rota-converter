If you are a junior doctor in the UK, use this site to convert your rota into a .csv file which can be imported into Google (or any other) Calendar. Simply:

  1. Select your hospital and specialty/department on the home page
  2. Upload your rota as an Excel file
  3. Specify your row/column and any other information requested
  4. Click 'convert!' and your converted rota will download as a csv file
  5. Congrats, you can now import the converted rota into Google calendar
  6. After importing into Google Calendar (for which all you need is a free Google Account), you can export from Google to get an .ics format. This .ics file can then be imported into Apple/iCloud Calendar or any other commonly used digital calendar service.
